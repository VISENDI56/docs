## Overview

Standard epidemiological models (SEIR, SIR) predict disease outbreaks. Tipping Point Early Warning (TP-EW) forecasting predicts **social and ecological collapse events** that cascade into health crises.

Using NVIDIA Modulus physics-informed AI, iLuminara simulates bifurcation points where minor perturbations trigger catastrophic system failures:
- Water shortage → social collapse → disease outbreak
- Biodiversity loss → zoonotic spillover → pandemic
- Climate tipping point → food system failure → malnutrition crisis

## The prediction gap

### Traditional epidemiology
- Models disease transmission dynamics
- Assumes stable social and environmental conditions
- Reactive to outbreaks after they begin

### Tipping point forecasting
- Models system-level instabilities
- Predicts when small changes trigger collapse
- Proactive intervention before crisis emerges

## Tipping point categories

### Ecological tipping points

**Zoonotic spillover cascade**
- Habitat fragmentation increases human-wildlife contact
- Biodiversity loss reduces natural disease regulation
- Climate stress forces animal migration into human settlements
- Result: Novel pathogen emergence

**Freshwater collapse**
- Aquifer depletion crosses critical threshold
- Agricultural failure triggers food insecurity
- Population displacement creates refugee crisis
- Result: Waterborne disease outbreak in displacement camps

### Social tipping points

**Conflict-health cascade**
- Resource scarcity increases social tension
- Minor incident triggers widespread violence
- Health infrastructure destroyed
- Result: Preventable disease mortality surge

**Economic collapse cascade**
- Currency devaluation crosses critical threshold
- Medical supply chains break down
- Healthcare workers emigrate
- Result: Health system failure

### Climate tipping points

**Heat stress cascade**
- Wet-bulb temperature exceeds human tolerance
- Mass heat casualties overwhelm hospitals
- Power grid failure disables cooling systems
- Result: Cascading mortality event

# Example PINN for water-health cascade
import modulus

class WaterHealthCascade(modulus.Module):
    def forward(self, x, t):
        # Neural network respects conservation of mass
        water_availability = self.net(x, t)
        
        # Physical constraint: water cannot be negative
        water_availability = torch.clamp(water_availability, min=0)
        
        # Predict health impact based on water stress
        health_risk = self.cascade_model(water_availability)
        
        return health_risk

## Early warning indicators

The system monitors leading indicators of instability:

| Indicator | Tipping point signal | Lead time |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------|
| Critical slowing down | System takes longer to recover from perturbations | 30-90 days |
| Increased variance | Fluctuations grow larger near bifurcation | 14-60 days |
| Spatial correlation | Local events synchronize across regions | 7-30 days |
| Flickering | Rapid switching between states | 3-14 days |

## Intervention strategies

### Pre-tipping point interventions

When the system approaches a tipping point but hasn't crossed it:
- **Resource pre-positioning** — Deploy medical supplies before crisis
- **Population stabilization** — Prevent displacement cascades
- **Infrastructure hardening** — Protect critical health facilities

### Post-tipping point response

After a tipping point is crossed:
- **Cascade containment** — Prevent spread to adjacent regions
- **Emergency stabilization** — Restore minimum viable services
- **Resilience building** — Prevent future tipping points
