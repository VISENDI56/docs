## Architecture philosophy

Each stack operates independently yet integrates seamlessly through the Golden Thread architecture, ensuring:

- **Sovereignty**: No external dependencies for critical operations
- **Resilience**: Graceful degradation when infrastructure fails
- **Compliance**: Built-in governance across 47 regulatory frameworks
- **Humanity**: Zero-knowledge privacy with humanitarian accountability

## The 10 architectural components

### 1. Biological intelligence layer
NVIDIA BioNeMo Evo 2 foundation model (9T nucleotide parameters) for zero-shot genomic prediction, protein design, and pandemic response.

**Hardware**: IGX Orin with FP8 quantization  
**Capability**: Generate heat-stable binders, predict variants, analyze pathogen sequences

### 2. Kinetic & sensory
NVIDIA Holoscan SDK (25h1) with cuOpt for real-time sensor processing and agentic logistics optimization.

**Hardware**: ConnectX-7 SmartNIC, Jetson AGX Orin  
**Capability**: Sub-millisecond medical imaging, natural language to VRP solving

### 3. Spatial omniscience
ESRI ArcGIS Native SDK with NVIDIA Modulus for offline mapping and physics-informed geospatial analysis.

**Hardware**: Edge devices with .vtpk caching  
**Capability**: P2P map sync, cholera runoff prediction, disaster response routing

### 4. Sovereign governance
Omni-Law Matrix enforcing 47 regulatory frameworks plus WHO Pandemic Accord Benefit-Sharing (PABS) protocol.

**Hardware**: Confidential computing enclaves  
**Capability**: Real-time compliance validation, genomic data sovereignty

### 5. Humanitarian substrate
Hyperledger Besu ReFi engine with Circom zero-knowledge proofs for privacy-preserving aid distribution.

**Hardware**: Distributed ledger nodes  
**Capability**: Bio-credit minting, eligibility verification without identity exposure

### 6. Connectivity & ghost-mesh
NVIDIA Aerial SDK (6G) with polymorphic mesh networking across 5G/LoRa/Wi-Fi Direct.

**Hardware**: ConnectX-7 with spectrum analysis  
**Capability**: Jamming-resistant communication, automatic protocol hopping

### 7. Educational sovereignty
Quantized Llama-3 8B (NIM) aligned with Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum for offline learning.

**Hardware**: Jetson Orin Nano  
**Capability**: Culturally-aligned lesson generation, peer-to-peer knowledge distribution

### 8. Agricultural & climate
NVIDIA Modulus physics-ML for micro-climate optimization in agro-voltaic installations.

**Hardware**: Edge inference on solar controllers  
**Capability**: Real-time PAR optimization, radiative transfer solving, crop yield prediction

### 9. Tele-justice
Confidential computing enclaves running Legal-LLM trained on 1951 Refugee Convention and humanitarian law.

**Hardware**: TEE-enabled processors  
**Capability**: Encrypted legal document generation, asylum claim processing

### 10. Circularity & autarky
cuOpt-powered reverse logistics for urban mining and e-waste harvesting to repair benches.

**Hardware**: Mobile routing nodes  
**Capability**: Zero-waste supply chains, component recovery optimization

## Integration patterns

All stacks communicate through:

- **Golden Thread**: Unified data lineage and provenance tracking
- **Resilient Mesh**: Adaptive P2P networking layer
- **Omni-Law**: Constitutional governance middleware
- **Bio-Credits**: Regenerative finance incentive layer
