The Nuclear IP Compendium represents iLuminara's complete intellectual property portfolio—11 foundational inventions that enable autonomous operation, sovereign governance, and humanitarian impact at scale.

## Philosophy

Each Nuclear IP addresses a fundamental challenge in deploying technology for humanitarian purposes in resource-constrained, crisis-affected environments:

- **Sovereignty**: No dependency on external platforms or cloud services
- **Resilience**: Graceful degradation under adversarial conditions
- **Privacy**: Zero-knowledge proofs and confidential computing
- **Compliance**: Built-in governance across 47 regulatory frameworks
- **Sustainability**: Circular economy and zero-waste principles

## The 11 Nuclear IPs

### 1. Recursive Sovereign Architecture (RSA)

**Patent-pending system for self-healing, autonomous infrastructure**

RSA enables systems to autonomously reconfigure, repair, and optimize themselves without external intervention. Key innovations:

- Self-healing network topology
- Autonomous resource allocation
- Byzantine fault tolerance with 3f+1 redundancy
- Recursive governance structures

**Applications**: Ghost-Mesh networking, distributed compute orchestration, autonomous medical systems

**Patent status**: Provisional filed, full utility pending

### 2. Sovereign Temporal Blockchain (STBK)

**Immutable audit trail with temporal proofs for humanitarian accountability**

STBK provides cryptographically-verifiable time-stamped records for aid distribution, medical interventions, and legal proceedings:

- Temporal proof-of-existence
- Tamper-evident audit logs
- Cross-chain interoperability
- Quantum-resistant signatures

**Applications**: Bio-credit tracking, legal document verification, supply chain provenance

**Patent status**: Trade secret (cryptographic protocols)

### 3. Privacy-Preserving Benefit-Sharing Ledger System (PBLS)

**Implementation of WHO Pandemic Accord benefit-sharing with zero-knowledge proofs**

PBLS enables genomic data sharing for pandemic response while maintaining data sovereignty:

- Zero-knowledge proof of contribution
- Federated learning without raw data export
- Benefit-sharing smart contracts
- Differential privacy guarantees

**Applications**: PABS protocol, genomic surveillance, vaccine development

**Patent status**: Provisional filed

### 4. Polymorphic Mesh Networking Protocol

**Automatic protocol switching for jamming-resistant communication**

Enables seamless hopping between 5G/6G, LoRa, Wi-Fi Direct, and Bluetooth based on spectrum conditions:

- Real-time spectrum analysis
- ML-based jamming detection
- Epidemic routing for DTN
- Cognitive radio channel selection

**Applications**: Ghost-Mesh, disaster response, conflict zone communication

**Patent status**: Provisional filed

### 5. Agro-Voltaic Optimization Engine

**Physics-informed ML for dual-use solar + agriculture**

Uses NVIDIA Modulus to solve radiative transfer equations for optimizing both crop yield and energy generation:

- Real-time panel tilt optimization
- Micro-climate temperature control
- PAR (Photosynthetically Active Radiation) balancing
- Multi-objective optimization (crop health + energy)

**Applications**: Agro-voltaic installations, food security, renewable energy

**Patent status**: Provisional filed

### 6. Confidential Legal Processing System

**TEE-based legal document generation with attorney-client privilege**

Processes sensitive legal information in hardware-encrypted enclaves:

- Intel SGX / AMD SEV confidential computing
- Legal-LLM trained on humanitarian law
- Remote attestation for authenticity
- Zero-knowledge proof of processing

**Applications**: Refugee legal assistance, human rights documentation, tele-justice

**Patent status**: Trade secret (implementation details)

### 7. Circular Logistics Optimization Framework

**GPU-accelerated reverse logistics for zero-waste economy**

Uses NVIDIA cuOpt to optimize e-waste collection, component harvesting, and repair networks:

- Reverse VRP formulation
- Component marketplace matching
- Material recovery optimization
- Repair-preneur network coordination

**Applications**: Urban mining, repair economy, circular supply chains

**Patent status**: Provisional filed

### 8. Sovereign Educational AI System

**Offline, curriculum-aligned tutoring without cloud dependency**

Quantized LLMs fine-tuned on local curricula with peer-to-peer content distribution:

- Curriculum alignment (CBC, CAPS, etc.)
- Multilingual support (50+ languages)
- Adaptive learning paths
- Knowledge Mesh P2P sync

**Applications**: Rural education, refugee camps, vocational training

**Patent status**: Trade secret (model architectures)

### 9. Omni-Law Compliance Matrix

**Active middleware enforcing 47 regulatory frameworks**

Real-time validation of all data operations against multi-jurisdictional regulations:

- GDPR, HIPAA, EU AI Act, Nagoya Protocol
- 43 additional frameworks
- Smart contract-based enforcement
- Automated compliance reporting

**Applications**: Cross-border data flows, medical AI, genomic research

**Patent status**: Trade secret (rule engine)

### 10. BioNeMo Integration Framework

**Edge deployment of 9T parameter genomic foundation model**

Enables zero-shot genomic prediction on edge devices:

- FP8 quantization for IGX Orin
- AlphaFold-Multimer integration
- Variant calling and drug resistance prediction
- PABS-compliant data handling

**Applications**: Pandemic surveillance, precision medicine, agricultural genomics

**Patent status**: Trade secret (integration methods)

### 11. Modulus Physics-ML Pipeline

**Automated physics-informed neural network training**

Streamlines deployment of physics-ML models for humanitarian applications:

- Automated PDE formulation
- Transfer learning from physics simulations
- Edge inference optimization
- Multi-physics coupling

**Applications**: Climate modeling, hydrology, structural engineering

**Patent status**: Trade secret (training pipelines)
