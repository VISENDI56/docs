## Use case & applicability matrix

Real-world applications of deep-tech engines in Dadaab 2026:

| Technical Singularity | Real-World Use Case | Impact |
|----------------------|---------------------|--------|
| **BioNeMo** (Generative Bio) | Drug-resistant cholera strain appears in Ifo 2 camp → BioNeMo designs protein binder on edge server | **Sovereign Bio-Defense**: Response time from months to hours |
| **cuOpt** (Kinetic Logistics) | Flash floods cut off main road → cuOpt re-calculates drone paths in 150ms, prioritizing insulin delivery | **Resilient Supply Chain**: 99% uptime during climate shocks |
| **Riva** (Semantic Voice) | Somali grandmother describes symptoms using rural idioms → Riva translates to SNOMED-CT codes | **Cultural Safety**: 40% reduction in misdiagnosis rates |
| **GeoGhost** (Offline Spatial) | Internet blackout during sandstorm → CHV tablet identifies sanitation risks locally | **Zero-Downtime Operations**: Health surveillance continues offline |
| **Shirika Equity Engine** | New water borehole drilled → Engine calculates fair usage schedule preventing conflict | **Social Cohesion**: Prevents resource-based violence |

## Core capabilities

### 1. Urban planning (Omniverse)

Transform camps into cities without displacing vulnerable people.

**Features:**
- Real-time 3D simulation of entire settlement
- Drag-and-drop infrastructure planning
- Flood risk analysis
- Disease spread modeling
- Social access optimization

**Impact:** Zero-risk urban redevelopment

### 2. Education (Knowledge Mesh)

Democratize high-quality education without internet costs.

**Features:**
- Quantized LLaMA-3 fine-tuned on Kenyan CBC Curriculum
- Multi-language support (English, Kiswahili, Somali)
- Offline-first operation on Ghost-Mesh
- Personalized learning paths
- Competency-based assessment

**Impact:** Teacher-to-student ratio from 1:100 to 1:10 (effective)
