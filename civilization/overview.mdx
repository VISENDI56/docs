## Use case & applicability matrix

Real-world applications in Dadaab 2026:

### BioNeMo (Generative Bio)

**Scenario:** Drug-resistant cholera strain appears in Ifo 2 camp

**Action:** BioNeMo designs specific protein binder on edge server (IGX Orin) without waiting for CDC Atlanta

**Impact:** Sovereign bio-defense - Response time from months to hours

### cuOpt (Kinetic Logistics)

**Scenario:** Flash floods cut off main road to Hagadera

**Action:** cuOpt re-calculates drone flight paths in 150ms, prioritizing insulin delivery to diabetic patients

**Impact:** Resilient supply chain - 99% uptime for life-saving drugs during climate shocks

### Riva (Semantic Voice)

**Scenario:** Somali grandmother describes symptoms using deep rural idioms (\"The fire in the bones\")

**Action:** Riva translates semantic nuance into standard SNOMED-CT codes while preserving cultural context

**Impact:** Cultural safety - 40% reduction in misdiagnosis rates

### GeoGhost (Offline Spatial)

**Scenario:** Internet blackout during sandstorm

**Action:** CHV tablet uses GeoGhost to identify open defecation risks in drone imagery locally

**Impact:** Zero-downtime operations - Health surveillance continues when grid fails

### Shirika Equity Engine

**Scenario:** New water borehole drilled - Garissa residents and refugees both claim it

**Action:** Engine calculates mathematically fair usage schedule based on population density and livestock needs

**Impact:** Social cohesion - Prevents resource-based violence
