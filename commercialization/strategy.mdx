## Business model

### Impact-as-a-Service (IaaS)

**Pricing structure:**

**Base infrastructure fee:**
- 50-node deployment: $48,000/year
- 500-node deployment: $240,000/year
- 5,000-node deployment: $480,000/year

**Outcome-based billing:**
- Outbreak prevented: $50,000 per incident
- Supply chain optimized: $10,000 per node per year
- Legal compliance verified: $5,000 per audit
- Wastage recovered: 10% of recovered value

**Revenue sharing model:**
- Organization keeps 80% of savings
- iLuminara receives 20% of verified savings
- Minimum annual fee covers infrastructure costs
- Upside unlimited based on impact

- Cost: $150,000 + 10% of verified savings

**Phase 3: National scale (12 months)**
- Deploy 500-node national network
- Full sovereignty transfer to government
- Autonomous operation with remote monitoring
- Cost: $500,000 + 15% of verified savings

### Operational risks

**Risk:** Difficulty scaling technical team
