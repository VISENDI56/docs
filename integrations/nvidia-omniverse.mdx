## Overview

NVIDIA Omniverse Digital Twin provides real-time 3D visualization and simulation for "Camp-to-City" urban planning under the Shirika Plan. It enables zero-risk urban redevelopment by simulating infrastructure changes before physical construction.

<Info>
  Under the Shirika Plan, Dadaab and Kalobeyei are transitioning from refugee camps to integrated municipalities. Omniverse enables this transformation without displacing vulnerable populations.
</Info>

## Civilization-scale singularity

**The Context:** Under the Shirika Plan, camps are becoming towns. This requires massive urban planning (roads, sewage, power) without displacing vulnerable people.

**The Singularity:** Omniverse USD (Universal Scene Description) Pipeline.

**Deep Architecture:** A real-time, photorealistic 3D simulation of the entire settlement. Planners can "drag and drop" a new clinic or market in the digital twin to see how it affects foot traffic, airflow (disease spread), and flood risks before building it.

**Impact:** Zero-risk urban redevelopment.

## Quick start

```python
from nvidia_integrations.omniverse_digital_twin import UrbanDigitalTwin

# Initialize Camp-to-City digital twin
twin = UrbanDigitalTwin(
    nucleus_server="omniverse://dadaab-planning",
    settlement_name="Dadaab",
    mode="camp_to_city"
)

# Load current settlement state
twin.load_settlement_usd("dadaab_2026_baseline.usd")

# Simulate new infrastructure
impact = twin.simulate_infrastructure_change(
    new_building_usd="market_complex_design.usd",
    location={"lat": 0.0512, "lng": 40.3129}
)

print(f"Flood risk change: {impact['flood_risk_delta']}")
print(f"Social access improvement: {impact['social_access_score']}")
print(f"Airflow impact (disease spread): {impact['airflow_coefficient']}")

# Test multiple scenarios
scenarios = twin.run_scenario_batch([
    {"type": "clinic", "capacity": 500},
    {"type": "school", "students": 1000},
    {"type": "water_station", "daily_liters": 50000}
])

# Select optimal configuration
optimal = twin.select_optimal_scenario(
    scenarios,
    criteria=["flood_resilience", "accessibility", "cost"]
)
```

## Integration with Golden Thread

## Real-world scenario (Dadaab 2026)

**Scenario:** The Shirika Plan requires converting Ifo 2 camp into a formal municipality with paved roads, sewage systems, and permanent structures.

**Challenge:** 50,000 residents cannot be displaced during construction. Planning must account for existing informal settlements, water sources, and social networks.

**Solution:** Omniverse Digital Twin simulates the entire transformation:

1. **Baseline scan:** Drone photogrammetry creates a 3D USD model of current Ifo 2 layout
2. **Infrastructure testing:** Planners drag-and-drop proposed roads, sewage lines, and power grids
3. **Impact analysis:** System calculates effects on foot traffic, flood drainage, and disease transmission
4. **Community review:** Residents view the simulation via VR headsets and provide feedback
5. **Iterative refinement:** Design is adjusted until all stakeholders approve

**Result:** Zero-displacement urban transformation completed in 18 months instead of 5 years.
