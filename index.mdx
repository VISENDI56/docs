### Framework summary

The 47 frameworks are organized into 8 categories:

- **Core Privacy & Data Protection** (14 frameworks) - GDPR, KDPA, HIPAA, POPIA, PIPEDA, CCPA, CPRA, LGPD, PDPA (SG), APPI, PIPL, PDPA (MY), DPD (UK), HITECH
- **Cybersecurity & Information Security** (8 frameworks) - NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, CIS Controls, MITRE ATT&CK, ISO 22301, NIST 800-53, COBIT
- **AI Ethics & Governance** (6 frameworks) - EU AI Act, IEEE Ethics, UNESCO AI, OECD AI, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF
- **Healthcare-Specific** (5 frameworks) - GDPR Art. 9, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, GxP Compliance, HIPAA Security Rule, ISO 27799
- **International Standards** (4 frameworks) - ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 31000
- **Regional & Sector-Specific** (8 frameworks) - African Union Data Policy, ASEAN Data Privacy, CARICOM, MERCOSUR, EFTA, APEC Privacy Framework, FTC Act, CAN-SPAM
- **Humanitarian & Ethical** (2 frameworks) - Geneva Convention, WHO IHR (2005)
