## Overview

iLuminara-Core is a compliance-first health intelligence platform designed for disease surveillance and outbreak response in resource-constrained environments.

Built for deployment across multiple jurisdictions, iLuminara enforces 47 global legal frameworks while maintaining consistent operation across different regulatory environments.

### Framework categories

The 47 frameworks are organized into 8 categories:

- **Core privacy and data protection** (14 frameworks) - GDPR, KDPA, HIPAA, POPIA, PIPEDA, CCPA, CPRA, LGPD, PDPA (SG), APPI, PIPL, PDPA (MY), DPD (UK), HITECH
- **Cybersecurity and information security** (8 frameworks) - NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, CIS Controls, MITRE ATT&CK, ISO 22301, NIST 800-53, COBIT
- **AI ethics and governance** (6 frameworks) - EU AI Act, IEEE Ethics, UNESCO AI, OECD AI, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF
- **Healthcare-specific** (5 frameworks) - GDPR Art. 9, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, GxP Compliance, HIPAA Security Rule, ISO 27799
- **International standards** (4 frameworks) - ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 31000
- **Regional and sector-specific** (8 frameworks) - African Union Data Policy, ASEAN Data Privacy, CARICOM, MERCOSUR, EFTA, APEC Privacy Framework, FTC Act, CAN-SPAM
- **Humanitarian and ethical** (2 frameworks) - Geneva Convention, WHO IHR (2005)
