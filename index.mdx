## Overview

iLuminara-Core is an **autonomous, post-quantum secure, offline-first AI system** delivering predictive, private, and provably compliant healthcare in contested environments.

**Living Law Singularity:** Fuses hardware (Blackwell B300), software (agentic governance), and international regulation (50 frameworks) into a single self-correcting entity that transforms refugee health from reactive crisis management into **predictive, ethical, sovereign care** — proven at camp scale.

Built for deployment across multiple jurisdictions, iLuminara enforces 50 global legal frameworks while maintaining consistent operation across different regulatory environments with **zero external cloud dependency**.

- **Cybersecurity and information security** (8 frameworks) - NIST CSF, ISO 27001, SOC 2, CIS Controls, MITRE ATT&CK, ISO 22301, NIST 800-53, COBIT
- **AI ethics and governance** (6 frameworks) - EU AI Act, IEEE Ethics, UNESCO AI, OECD AI, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF
- **Healthcare-specific** (5 frameworks) - GDPR Art. 9, FDA 21 CFR Part 11, GxP Compliance, HIPAA Security Rule, ISO 27799
- **International standards** (4 frameworks) - ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 31000
- **Regional and sector-specific** (10 frameworks) - African Union Data Policy, ASEAN Data Privacy, CARICOM, MERCOSUR, EFTA, APEC Privacy Framework, FTC Act, CAN-SPAM, Kenya Digital Health Act, Malabo Convention
- **Humanitarian and ethical** (3 frameworks) - 1951 Refugee Convention, Geneva Convention, WHO IHR (2005)
