Winning a Nobel Prize requires more than "optimizing" a system—it requires solving a class of problems previously thought unsolvable. DeepMind won the Chemistry Nobel because AlphaFold solved the 50-year-old "Protein Folding Problem."

iLuminara targets the **"Last Mile of Humanity" Problem**—maintaining civilization, health, and dignity in disconnected, resource-scarce, and climate-hostile environments (Dadaab/Kalobeyei 2026).
