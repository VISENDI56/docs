**Architecture:**
- Use **NVIDIA Modulus** to model the "Viral Chatter" between wildlife and humans at water points
- Water points monitored by **GeoGhost**
- Predict spillover events before they occur

### The DeepMind Moment
Predicting a spillover event **T-minus 14 days before patient zero**. iLuminara dispatches cuOpt drones to vaccinate the animals (reservoir hosts) at the water source, stopping the pandemic before it jumps to humans.

```python
class PandemicShield:

    Metagenomic forecasting for pre-emptive pandemic prevention.
    """

        # Sequence viral RNA

        if spillover_risk > 0.7:
            # Pre-emptive intervention
            self.dispatch_animal_vaccination(water_point_id)
            
        return spillover_risk
```
